NetBeans 10th Birthday Celebration: NetBeans IDE Over Time

In Summer 1998, Roman Stanek, Ian Formánek, Yarda Tulach, and Helena Stolka founded the NetBeans project. The NetBeans user interface developed and changed greatly over time: Have a look at our collection of historic screenshots — do you remember?
