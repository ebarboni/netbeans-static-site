NetBeans 10th Birthday Celebration: NetBeans Logos Over Time

It's an exclusive! A first public look at the earliest NetBeans logos! The images below were taken from framed pictures on the walls of the Prague office. These are the original first drafts of the NetBeans logo—what it was meant to look like, but never did!

So the bean evolved into a cube, but the change didn't stop there: Check out the different stages of the NetBeans cube from NetBeans IDE 4 and 5 to what it presently looks like for NetBeans IDE 6.1, and what it will look like for NetBeans IDE 6.5.
