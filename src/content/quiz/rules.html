NO PURCHASE NECESSARY

DESCRIPTION OF THE SWEEPSTAKES:

The NetBeans Quiz Sweepstakes (“Sweepstakes”) offers entrants the chance to win a prize by participating in weekly quizzes submitted through the NetBeans Update Center. The details of the prizes and how to enter the Sweepstakes are set forth in these Official Rules.

ELIGIBILITY:

This Sweepstakes is open only to legal residents of India who have reached the age of majority at the time of entry. Void where prohibited by law. Employees of Sun Microsystems, Inc., and its parent companies, affiliates and subsidiaries, participating advertising and promotion agencies (and members of their immediate family, defined as parents, children, siblings and spouse, regardless of where they reside, and/or those living in the same household) are not eligible. Entrants must also have access to the Internet and a valid email address in order to enter.

HOW TO ENTER:

During the period from April 14, 2008 at 12:01 PM India Standard Time (“IST”) through June 30, 2008 at 11:59 PM IST (the “Sweepstakes Period”), entrants will be automatically entered into a weekly drawing when they submit correct answers to all of the questions on a weekly NetBeans quiz. (“Weekly Quiz”). The Sweepstakes consists of 11 weekly entry periods (“Weekly Entry Periods”). Each Weekly Entry Period will begin on Monday at 12:01 PM IST and end on Monday at 11:59 PM IST. The Sponsor’s clock is the official time clock of the Sweepstakes. Proof of submission does not constitute proof of receipt.

You must have NetBeans 5.5 or later installed on your computer, and install the optional NetBeans Quiz plug-in module from the NetBeans Update Center to participate in each Weekly Quiz. NetBeans 6.0 is available for free at www.netbeans.org. Install NetBeans, including the NetBeans quiz module and follow the instructions to complete and submit a registration form for the Sweepstakes. You will be required to provide your name, email address, and mailing address.

Once you have registered for the Sweepstakes, you may opt to receive a weekly email notification with a link to the Weekly Quiz. The Weekly Quiz will consist of technical software development themed questions. All required fields of the Weekly Quiz entry form must be filled out, including your answer to the Weekly Quiz. Additionally, to be eligible for a particular Weekly Entry Period’s Drawing, your answer must: (1) be provided in English; (2) be provided in the form specified by the particular Weekly Quiz; and (3) must provide correct answers to all questions in the particular Weekly Quiz. (“Entry”). Limit one (1) Entry per person or email address per Weekly Entry Period. If an entrant uses multiple email addresses to submit more than one Entry for a given Weekly Entry Period, only the first Entry will be eligible for that Weekly Entry Period’s drawing. (“Weekly Drawing”). Entries will not roll over to subsequent Weekly Entry Period Drawings. Odds of winning Weekly Prize depends on the number of eligible Entries received.

The “Cumulative Grand Prize” will be awarded to the entrant who has answered the most Weekly Quiz questions correctly during the Sweepstakes Period. In the event of ties, the Cumulative Grand Prize winner will be determined in a random drawing from among those entrants who are eligible to receive the Cumulative Grand Prize.

PRIZES:

Grand Prize:

There will be One (1) Grand Prize awarded, consisting of an Apple MacBook Air laptop computer. Approximate Retail Value (“ARV”) is $1799.00 USD.

Weekly Prizes:

There will be 11 Weekly Prizes awarded. Weekly Prize will vary on a weekly basis, including, possibly iPods, TV’s, Play Stations, Laptops and a Home Entertainment Center.
