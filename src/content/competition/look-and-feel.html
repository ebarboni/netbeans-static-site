NetBeans Look and Feel Competition

Have you customised your NetBeans IDE Look & Feel ? Are you using a non-standard Look&Feel, or are you using the Substance plug-in to change NetBeans' look ? Have you tweaked the L&F in some other way ? These NetBeans community members have shared their customisations and won one of three iPod Nanos, 10 USB flash memory sticks or 30 NetBeans T-Shirts!

This competition is closed and the winners have been informed. Many thanks to all participants!

Here are the Look&Feel Competition winners. Most of these customisations were created with the substance plugin - see the appropriate guides linked from the right column on how to do it!
