In the event of a tie, the person among the tied Entries that best highlights how NetBeans IDE 6.1 Beta or NetBeans IDE 6.1 RC1 features are used in building an application will be awarded the grand prize. In the event that no entries are received, no prize will be awarded. Decisions of judges are final and binding. Winner will be notified by email.
